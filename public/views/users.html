Please read this before you start
  • Add employees who actively use Punctoo.
  • After the first scan, employees can no longer be deleted (Raw data present).
  • To keep a correct overview, you can switch an employee from Active to Inactive.
  • Stored raw data: Employee Code, IN-scan timestamp, OUT-scan timestamp.
  • Working schedules are optional and only used for Over-Time calculations in reports.
  • Working schedules can be added or removed at any time.
  • Employees without a working schedule will not be included in the Over-Time report.
